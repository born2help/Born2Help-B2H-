Who We Are

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is a humanitarian-first foundation. Every effort is converted into measurable, sustainable aid. Ethics, responsibility, and long-term impact guide all decisions.

“Technology should serve humanity without compromising ethics.”

— Mubashir Munnawar

Our Vision

A world where relief, dignity, education, and care persist regardless of wealth or economic conditions — systems designed to serve generations.

Our Mission

Transform contributions into sustainable mechanisms that continuously serve humanity, creating true Sadaqah Jariyah for participants and beneficiaries alike.

Core Values

  • Integrity — over shortcuts or speed
  • Trust — over hype or trends
  • Patience — over instant gratification
  • Humanity — over profit or popularity
  • Transparency — within ethical and safe boundaries

Impact: Over 500,000 people reached through humanitarian initiatives, ethical relief systems, and long-term assistance programs.

Founder’s Intention

B2H was founded by Mubashir Munnawar with one principle: technology must serve humanity without personal gain. Every decision is made with patience, accountability, and ethical responsibility.
