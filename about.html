Why Donate to B2H?

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is not built on emotion, trends, or short-term campaigns. It is designed as a long-term humanitarian structure where every contribution strengthens a system meant to serve humanity continuously.

What Your Contribution Supports

  • Direct humanitarian relief and dignity protection
  • Ethical infrastructure and long-term sustainability
  • Transparent operational maintenance
  • Permanent burn mechanisms tied to humanitarian impact

No Ownership. No Control. No Influence.

Donations do not grant voting rights, ownership, or authority. B2H remains mission-locked, ethically governed, and protected from capital pressure.

How to Donate

Donation mechanisms will be published transparently through official B2H channels. All methods will comply with ethical safeguards and mission integrity.

⚠️ Never send funds to unofficial addresses or intermediaries.
