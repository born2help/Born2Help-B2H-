Who We Are

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is a humanitarian-first foundation. We ensure that helping people is never dependent on trends, emotions, or market cycles. Ethics, responsibility, and long-term impact guide every decision.

Our Vision

A world where systems are designed to continuously help humanity — where relief, dignity, education, and care persist regardless of wealth or economic conditions.

Our Mission

To convert contributions into sustainable mechanisms that serve humanity continuously, creating a true Sadaqah Jariyah for both participants and beneficiaries.

Founder’s Intention

B2H was founded by Mubashir Munnawar on a simple belief: technology should serve humanity without compromising ethics. No personal financial gain exists — every decision is made with patience, accountability, and responsibility before Allah.

Core Values

  • Integrity — over speed and shortcuts
  • Trust — over hype and noise
  • Patience — over pressure and instant results
  • Humanity — over profit and popularity
  • Transparency — within ethical and safe boundaries

Our Commitment

B2H protects dignity and ensures sustainable relief for the most vulnerable. Every structure, system, and process is designed for long-term stewardship — helping not just for years, but for generations.
