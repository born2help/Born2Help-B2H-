Token Overview

  • Token Name: B2H
  • Meaning: Burn to Help · Born for Humanity
  • Total Supply: 500,000,000,000 B2H
  • Supply Type: Fixed — No future minting

Fixed Supply Principle

B2H has a permanently fixed supply. No additional tokens can ever be created. This ensures predictability, prevents dilution, and supports long-term trust.

Burn-to-Help Mechanism

The Burn-to-Help mechanism permanently removes tokens from circulation while triggering predefined allocations toward humanitarian initiatives and project sustainability.

Burns are not designed to manipulate price or guarantee value. They exist to align participation with social impact.

Allocation Philosophy

  • Humanitarian assistance and aid programs (5%)
  • Operational sustainability and infrastructure (3%)
  • Security, compliance, and ethical governance
  • Minimal administration and coordination

What Tokenomics Do NOT Promise

  • No guaranteed returns
  • No price appreciation targets
  • No profit-sharing claims
  • No speculative incentives

Participation Ethics

Participation in B2H is voluntary and should be based on alignment with humanitarian values — not financial expectations.

Founder Stewardship

Tokenomics decisions are guided by ethical responsibility. The founder retains stewardship authority to protect the mission, not to extract personal benefit.

Risk Disclosure

Participation involves risk. B2H does not guarantee financial outcomes. Individuals should engage only with full understanding of its humanitarian nature.
