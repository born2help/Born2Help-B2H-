Governance

B2H operates under a transparent governance system. All key decisions are recorded on the blockchain, ensuring no single entity can control the project. Contributors have voting rights on major decisions, enabling a decentralized and accountable ecosystem. This ensures integrity, fairness, and long-term sustainability of the platform.

Constitution

Our constitution protects charity funds, the burn wallet, and overall project governance. Every transaction is regulated under strict rules to maintain transparency, sustainability, and maximum humanitarian impact. It ensures ethical operations, accountability, and long-term trust for all participants and beneficiaries.
