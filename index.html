How B2H Helps Humanity

Every token purchase and sale contributes to charity. From providing food, medicine, water, and shelter to supporting education and marriages for the needy, B2H ensures real impact with every transaction.

Even if you invest a single unit, you are part of a global movement to help humanity while also earning rewards from your investment.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 500,000,000,000 tokens
  • Burn Mechanism: Every transaction contributes a % to charity and burn wallet
  • Owner/Company Reserve: 20% for operational, travel, office, and personal expenses
  • Buy/Sell Tax: Ensures sustainability and continuous support for charity
  • Accessible for Everyone: Designed so even small investors can participate

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Launch website, token contract, community setup
  • Phase 2: Social media outreach, charity partnerships, initial listings
  • Phase 3: Expanded charity impact, collaborations with philanthropists and governments
  • Phase 4: Full-scale global adoption, professional campaigns, B2H as a historic crypto project

Transparency & Protection

All transactions are recorded on the blockchain. Charity wallet and burn wallet are public. Owner cannot access charity funds directly. Security audits ensure no one can hijack this project.
