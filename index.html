Message from the Founder

B2H was founded on one belief: wealth should heal humanity, not divide it. Our mission is not temporary charity, but sustainable impact that lives on for generations.

My name is Mubashir Munnawar.
B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) was born from a simple belief: that humanity can use technology responsibly to serve people in need.

This project is built on patience, transparency, and long-term responsibility. There are no guaranteed returns, no hidden agendas, and no rush. Participation, if any, is voluntary and based on shared values—not promises.

I am not a wealthy person, but I believe sincerity, accountability, and faith in doing the right thing matter more than capital. My commitment is to protect the integrity of this project for as long as I am able.

B2H exists to explore a new idea: that technology can be structured to help humanity while remaining ethical, lawful, and transparent.

Born to Help is built as a living system of compassion. Every donation is converted into long-term assets, and the profit generated is continuously used to uplift the poor — a true Sadaqah-e-Jariyah.

These assets are never sold. Their benefits continue to serve humanity, ensuring dignity, transparency, and accountability in every action we take.

Together, we are building a foundation where generosity becomes eternal impact.

— Founder, B2H Global Foundation
