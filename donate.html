Donate with Purpose

Your contribution is an act of service — not an investment

B2H donations are intended to support humanitarian initiatives, long-term sustainability, and ethical operations. Donations are not speculative, refundable, or profit-generating.

What Your Donation Supports

  • Humanitarian relief and aid programs
  • Education, healthcare, and food security initiatives
  • Operational sustainability of B2H
  • Compliance, protection, and responsible coordination

What Donations Are NOT

  • Not an investment
  • Not a promise of financial return
  • Not tied to token price or market activity
  • Not a speculative contribution

Donations are accepted only from individuals who understand and agree with B2H’s humanitarian-first principles.

Sadaqah Jariyah

B2H is designed to allow participation as a form of Sadaqah Jariyah — continuous charity that benefits people over time.

Donation Methods

Approved donation methods will be published transparently when active. Any request for private, direct, or unofficial payments should be treated as misinformation.

Important Notice

Anyone promising guaranteed returns, special access, or financial benefits does not represent B2H.
