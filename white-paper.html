Overview

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is a humanitarian-first blockchain initiative designed to translate digital participation into tangible, lasting impact. Unlike speculative tokens, B2H focuses on creating real-world humanitarian value while ensuring transparency, accountability, and ethical stewardship.

Vision & Mission

Vision: A world where helping others is built into systems, creating lasting relief and opportunities for all, irrespective of wealth or geography.

Mission: To transform contributions into sustainable, income-generating assets that continuously serve humanity, creating a true Sadaqah Jariyah for participants.

Tokenomics

The B2H token is designed for ethical participation and maximum humanitarian impact:

  • Total Supply: 500 billion B2H tokens, fixed supply, no future minting.
  • Burn-to-Help Mechanism: Defined token burns reduce supply while funding aid.
  • Transparent Allocation: Charity (5%), Operations (3%), Permanent Burn (2%), Community Supply (90%).

Every token holder participates in a system where their engagement directly supports verified humanitarian initiatives.

Ethical Framework

B2H is built on principles that prioritize humanity over profit:

  • Transparency in all operations and reporting
  • No personal gain for founders or trustees
  • Faith-aligned financial practices (no interest, no Riba)
  • Continuous oversight by a rotating ethical council

Long-Term Impact

Contributions to B2H are transformed into permanent assets that generate continuous support for the poor and vulnerable. By structuring our model ethically and sustainably, every action—from token burns to project execution—delivers measurable, traceable humanitarian impact.

How to Participate

Participation in B2H is voluntary, transparent, and open to all. There are no guarantees of financial return. Instead, contributors gain the satisfaction of knowing their engagement creates a living system of good—an impact that persists across generations.

Our Commitment

B2H is more than a token; it is a humanitarian instrument. We are committed to long-term stewardship, transparency, and ethical management. Every participant, donor, and community beneficiary shares in a vision where technology serves humanity with integrity and compassion.
