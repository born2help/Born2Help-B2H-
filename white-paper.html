Overview

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) transforms participation into lasting humanitarian benefit.

Built on patience, transparency, ethical restraint, and long-term responsibility, B2H demonstrates that technology can protect dignity and deliver sustainable impact.

Purpose & Core Values

  • Embed compassion and responsibility into technology
  • Support verified humanitarian initiatives
  • Build systems that outlive individuals
  • Maintain ethical transparency

Core Values: Integrity over speed · Trust over hype · Humanity over profit

Token Overview

  • Name: B2H
  • Total Supply: 500,000,000,000
  • Supply Type: Fixed (no inflation)

Burn-to-Help Mechanism

Burn events permanently reduce supply while activating humanitarian allocations — aligning participation with responsibility, not speculation.

Transparency & Ethics

  • No promised profits
  • No price speculation
  • Clear separation of funds
  • No founder enrichment

Vision & Mission

Vision: Long-lasting humanitarian systems.

Mission: Create true Sadaqah Jariyah through technology.

Ethical Framework

  • Transparency at all levels
  • Faith-aligned practices
  • Independent oversight

Long-Term Impact

Contributions become permanent assets supporting communities across generations.

Our Commitment

B2H is a humanitarian instrument — not a speculative product. Stewardship, ethics, and transparency are permanent.
